## Shipping

### UK shipping

- **Free next-day delivery on orders over £50.** Dispatched same day if ordered before 15:00 Monday to Friday. DPD or Royal Mail Tracked 24 depending on size.
- **Orders under £50.** £4.99 next-day, £2.99 standard (2–3 working days, Royal Mail Tracked 48).
- **Heavy items (full desktop towers over 15kg, monitors over 27").** £9.99 next-day DPD, regardless of order value, because these ship on a separate courier tier.
- **Saturday delivery.** £9.99 supplement if ordered by 15:00 Friday.
- **Scottish Highlands and Northern Ireland.** Next-day upgrades to two-day — courier limitation, not our choice. No additional charge.

Evri is used for small parts and accessories (cables, adapters, RAM sticks) where tracking at this price point is sufficient. Anything over £100 in value ships DPD or Royal Mail Tracked as a minimum.

### International shipping

We ship to the EU, EEA, Switzerland, USA, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, UAE, Singapore, and Hong Kong. Customers are responsible for import duty and VAT at the destination — we cannot pre-pay these for most destinations.

- **EU.** £14.99 flat rate for small items, £29.99 for desktops and monitors. 3–5 working days via DPD International.
- **USA / Canada.** £24.99 small, £59.99 desktop. 4–7 working days via FedEx or DHL.
- **Australia / NZ.** £34.99 small, £89.99 desktop. 5–9 working days.
- **Middle East and APAC.** Quoted per order.

### Tracking

Every order gets a tracking number by email the moment the label is generated. The tracking page works without a customer account. If tracking isn't updating 24 hours after dispatch, support can investigate directly with the courier.

## Returns

### The 14-day no-questions return

Every order from Birmingham AV can be returned within 14 days of delivery, for any reason, for a full refund. This covers change-of-mind as well as faults. Goods must be in a re-saleable condition — in the original packaging, with accessories, and in substantially the same condition they arrived in.

The process:

1. Start the return from your account area or by emailing support with the order number.
2. We send a DPD return label (free for UK customers, chargeable for international).
3. Pack the item in the original box, attach the label, drop at any DPD point or book a collection.
4. Once the item is back on the bench and inspected, the refund is issued to the original payment method.

### Refund timings

- **Card (Visa / Mastercard / Amex).** 3–5 working days to appear on the statement after we issue.
- **PayPal.** Same day, usually within hours.
- **Apple Pay / Google Pay.** Same as the underlying card — 3–5 working days.
- **Klarna / Clearpay.** Issued to Klarna / Clearpay the same working day; they remove the plan within 24 hours.
- **BACS / invoice.** Refunded to the originating bank account within 5 working days.

### What doesn't fall under 14-day returns

- **Software licences that have been activated.** Windows 11 retail keys, Office keys, Adobe subscriptions — once activated, the vendor considers them consumed and we cannot return them.
- **Customised builds where the specification was customer-requested.** We stand behind faults under warranty but cannot take back a one-off build for change-of-mind reasons. We flag this clearly on the configurator before checkout.
- **Consumables.** Thermal paste tubes that have been opened, cleaning sprays, and similar.

Everything else — including built-to-order PCs with standard configurations — is returnable within 14 days.

### Faulty items

Anything that arrives faulty, or develops a fault within the warranty period, is covered under the 12-month warranty rather than the 14-day return. In practice the early process is the same: contact support, send it back, we diagnose. Where the 14-day window still applies, we usually refund rather than repair — it's faster for everyone.

## Warranty

Every product — new or refurbished — ships with a **12-month warranty** covering component faults. This is additional to any manufacturer warranty the part carries. The longer of the two applies; customers never lose coverage by buying from us.

### What it covers

- Any component failure under normal use — GPU dying, SSD failing, motherboard refusing to POST, PSU shutting down under load.
- Manufacturing defects that develop over the 12 months — backlight bleed beyond normal, dead pixels, fan bearings failing.
- Firmware issues that require a rework (BIOS corruption on a board we sold).

### What it doesn't cover

- Physical damage — drops, spills, power surges. These are covered by AV Care; see the AV Care section below.
- Wear-and-tear consumables — thermal paste drying out after 3+ years, laptop battery degradation within normal bounds (we replace laptop batteries under warranty only if they fall below 80% of design capacity within 12 months).
- Software issues — virus infections, driver problems from third-party installs, Windows activation after a customer has rebuilt the machine.
- Modifications a customer has made. Overclocking, custom cooling, BIOS modifications — if the fault is related to those, it's not a warranty case. If the fault is unrelated to the modification, it still is.

### How it works in practice

Contact support, describe the fault, and we either fix it remotely (driver, BIOS, Windows issue), collect and repair (most hardware faults), or replace the unit outright (the diagnosis takes longer than the replacement would). Collections and returns under warranty are always paid by us — no customer pays postage for a warranty claim.

Turnaround:

- Remote fix — same day or next day.
- Hardware diagnosis and repair — 5–10 working days from collection.
- Full replacement — same or equivalent unit dispatched within 3 working days.

## Trade-in

We buy back PCs, laptops, monitors, and components. Many customers trade in an older machine when buying a new one — the trade-in value comes off the new order and payment clears the same working day the inspection finishes.

### The process

1. Submit a trade-in application with specs and photos at `/trade-in`.
2. We respond within one working day with an indicative offer range, based on current market rates and our resale margin.
3. You either pack the unit yourself and send it via a prepaid label, or we book a free UK collection.
4. The unit arrives, passes through inbound inspection on the same bench as our refurbishment queue.
5. We confirm the final offer in writing. If it matches the indicative, we process payment same-day.
6. If it differs (the spec wasn't quite as described, or we spot a fault), we explain precisely why and offer the revised figure. You can accept, or ask for the unit back — free return if you don't want to proceed.

### The offer guarantee

The indicative offer is the offer unless something material differs from the application. We don't re-quote downward for cosmetic wear we could see in the submitted photos, and we don't re-quote for "market conditions changed" between the application and the unit arriving. The only revisions are genuine spec differences or genuine undisclosed faults.

### Payment

- **BACS bank transfer.** Same working day after inspection completes. Most common.
- **Store credit.** 10% uplift on the cash offer. Credit sits against the account and can be applied to any future order.
- **Against a new build or order.** Trade-in value credits directly against the order; balance charged to your card.

### What we don't accept

- Units beyond economic repair (water damage, substantial motherboard damage, cracked CPU or GPU substrate).
- Mining GPUs sold as gaming cards — we accept ex-mining cards, priced appropriately and sold as ex-mining, but not dressed up.
- Machines older than 10 years unless they're specifically collectible (a working ThinkPad X220, a Power Mac G5 for a retro project). The market for the rest isn't there.

## Business accounts

Trade customers — resellers, IT services companies, agencies, studios, schools, and local authorities — get a separate account structure.

### What's included

- **Trade pricing.** Typically 5–12% below consumer pricing depending on category and volume. Always a genuine discount against the live consumer price, not dressed-up RRP.
- **Quoted bulk pricing.** Orders of 10+ units of the same SKU get individual quotes with further volume discount. Most orders over £10,000 quote at 12–18% below consumer, depending on margin on the components.
- **Dedicated account manager.** Named person, direct email, direct phone. Quotes typically back within two hours in normal business times.
- **Net-30 on approval.** Net-60 on trading history.
- **VAT invoicing.** Clean invoices with purchase order references, itemised for procurement systems.
- **White-label despatch.** Orders can ship direct to your customer with your branding on the delivery note, no Birmingham AV branding on the outer.
- **Configurable preferred specifications.** We hold your standard builds on file — a school's standard classroom PC, an agency's standard editing workstation — for fast reorder.
- **Preferential AV Care Plus pricing.** See below.

At `/business`, submit company details and a VAT number. We respond within one working day with a credit decision and account credentials.

## AV Care — the subscription warranty

AV Care is Birmingham AV's above-warranty care programme. It sits on top of the standard 12-month warranty and runs continuously from day one. It covers what the standard warranty doesn't, and it keeps running after the standard warranty ends.

### How it's structured

- **Per-account billing.** One subscription covers every Birmingham AV product currently registered to your account. We don't bill per-product or per-device.
- **£100 excess per claim.** Flat. The same on both tiers, the same whether the claim is for a £400 laptop or a £4,000 workstation.
- **30-day free trial.** Either tier. If you cancel inside the trial, nothing is charged.
- **Monthly or annual billing.** Annual is roughly two months free — we quote annual prices openly so customers can compare.
- **Cancel any time.** Month-to-month on monthly plans, pro-rata refund on annual.

### Essential — £14.99 / month or £149 / year

Everything the standard warranty misses:

- **Accidental damage.** Drops, spills, power-surge damage, cracked screens. None of those are covered by the standard manufacturer or refurbishment warranty; all are covered by Essential.
- **Free UK collection and return** when a claim is raised. We pay both legs via DPD. No customer ever pays postage under AV Care.
- **48-hour diagnostic SLA** from the moment the unit lands on the bench. You know within two working days what's wrong and what the resolution plan is.
- **Software support line.** Driver issues, Windows activation problems, BIOS flashing, boot repair, general "it was working and now it's not" queries. Handled by the team directly, no ticket queue.
- **Runs forever while subscribed.** The standard warranty expires at 12 months. AV Care doesn't. A product bought in 2024, still on Essential in 2029, is still covered.
- **£100 excess per claim.**

### Plus — £29.99 / month or £299 / year

Everything in Essential, with priority treatment:

- **Loan device during repair.** A mid-range laptop or a mid-range desktop from the AV Care loaner pool, shipped the same day your unit is collected. You don't stop working while the repair runs.
- **Cross-shipping.** If the failed part is in stock, the replacement ships to you *before* we receive the faulty unit back. You fit the replacement, ship us the original, no downtime on the customer side.
- **Annual preventative service.** One scheduled visit per subscription year — thermal repaste, fan clean, burn-in re-test, cable tidy, firmware updates applied. Returned in "as-new" condition. The bench picks up the courier both ways.
- **Component upgrade offset.** If your part has failed and an identical replacement isn't available — common with older GPUs and CPUs — we apply 100% of the original part's value toward the next-tier equivalent. You never pay the shortfall. The programme covers it.
- **Priority queue.** AV Care Plus jobs are pushed ahead of standard workshop jobs. Your Plus claim jumps the queue.
- **24-hour diagnostic SLA** rather than 48.
- **£100 excess per claim.**

### What's not covered on either tier

- Theft
- Intentional damage
- Cosmetic wear that doesn't affect function (scratches on a case, marks on a keyboard after years of use)
- Unauthorised modifications (overclocking that killed a CPU, custom loops that leaked)
- Software licence fees — Windows, Office, Adobe subscriptions, game licences. We can help you through activation issues, but we don't cover the cost of the licence itself.

### How to raise a claim

From the account area, `/account/av-care` → Raise a claim. Describe the fault, add a photo, pick a collection date. The £100 excess is charged at the point we confirm the claim is valid (not up front). The claim status is visible in real time in the account area from submission through to return delivery.
